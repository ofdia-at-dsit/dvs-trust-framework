## 8. Rules for orchestration service providers

8.a. Orchestration service providers must follow these rules and the rules in [Part 3](../part-3/README.md#part-3).

<a id="section-8_1"></a>

### 8.1. Reporting the DVS registration of others

8.1.a. You must have a mechanism to confirm whether any given service you orchestrate is listed on the register.

8.1.b. If this confirmation is made with reference to a saved record (or ‘cache’) of information from the register, you must have a policy for how often you update this cache. For example, your policy could be that this cache is updatedas regularly as the register itself. 

8.1.c. If you agree to provide this confirmation to a relying party or another DVS and you make this confirmation with reference to a cache, you must agree with them how recent your cache will be at minimum for any given check.
