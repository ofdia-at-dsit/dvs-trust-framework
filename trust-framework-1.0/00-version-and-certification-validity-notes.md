## 0. Version and certification validity notes
<a id="section-0"></a>

0.a. This 1.0 publication of the UK digital verification services trust framework (‘trust framework’) will come into force on [date of final publication]. Any new certifications or recertifications after this date must be made against this publication or another valid publication of the trust framework. 

0.b. All previous publications of the trust framework were titled the ‘UK digital identity and attributes trust framework’. As the first revised statutory trust framework publication under the Data (Use and Access) Act 2025, we have aligned the title of this publication with the term given to the trust framework in the legislation: ‘DVS trust framework’. These two titles refer to different versions of the same document.

0.c. All certifications against the beta (0.3) version of the trust framework expired on 31 March 2026. Certifications against the gamma (0.4) publication of the trust framework will remain valid until [date to be confirmed in final publication] or one of the following conditions is met:

- a service uplifts to the 1.0 publication; or

- the service’s gamma (0.4) certification expires.
